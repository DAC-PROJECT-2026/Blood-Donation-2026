Do's & Don'ts · Declaration
Please read carefully before donating blood
✅ Do's — Before Donating
  • 💧 Drink plenty of water (at least 2–3 glasses)
  • 🍽️ Eat a light, nutritious meal 2–3 hours before
  • 😴 Get a good night's sleep (minimum 7 hours)
  • 👕 Wear clothing with sleeves that roll up easily
  • 🆔 Carry your employee ID to the venue
  • 🧘 Stay relaxed — deep breaths help!
  • 🪑 Rest for at least 15 minutes after donating
  • 🍪 Have a snack/juice provided at the camp post-donation
🚫 Don'ts — Before & After
  • 🚬 No smoking for at least 2 hours before and after
  • 🍺 No alcohol for 24 hours before donation
  • 🏋️ Avoid heavy exercise or strenuous activity on the day
  • ☕ Skip caffeinated drinks (tea/coffee) before donating
  • 🍔 Avoid fatty or oily food before the donation
  • 🛵 Do not drive two-wheelers immediately after donating
  • 💊 Do not donate if on antibiotics or blood thinners
  • 🤧 Do not donate if you have cold, fever, or infection
